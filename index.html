About Gecko.mn

Gecko.mn is committed to advancing blockchain technology in line with Satoshi Nakamoto's vision. Its focus on decentralization and transparency ensures a more secure digital economy. Learn more at gecko.mn.

Ghostrider Algorithm

The Ghostrider algorithm combines the efficiency of multiple hashing functions to ensure fairness, defeating ASIC dominance. Its adaptive design makes it ideal for CPU mining, embodying Satoshi's "one CPU, one vote" philosophy.

X16R Algorithm

With dynamic algorithms, X16R minimizes ASIC advantage, promoting decentralization. Its versatility ensures long-term sustainability for miners and the ecosystem.
